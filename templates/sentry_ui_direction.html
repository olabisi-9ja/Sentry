Direction

Threads' rhythm — pure black canvas, quiet dividers, one verified account cutting through the noise — applied to community reports instead of social posts.

Palette

Black + teal carries the brand. Category colors are functional, not decorative — each one is a live legend for the map and heatmap later.

Type

Sora for the wordmark and headers — geometric, confident. System sans for report text — has to read instantly at 2am. JetBrains Mono for anything numeric: confidence, timestamps, risk scores — signals "this is measured data," not opinion.

Signature move

Confidence, not likes
